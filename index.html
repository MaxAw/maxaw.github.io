Dear Ila,
I'm very excited that you are growing older. I am a bit of an ageist that way. I believe the better years in life are awaiting us always we can always leave a lot of shit behind with our youth. Like snakes shedding old dead skin. I like to tell myself that.

I've spent too much time with middle aged people since I left uni and this is all probably coming from a need to fit in. I've been patronised up and down and involuntarily made representative of my "generation". But I've also been eating only good food and been listening to only good music, and I'm making home made butter and it was so good. And I owe it to my middle aged friends.

But of course I'm not shedding dead skin the way I want to. I don't think I can. And the closer I look at my older friends the more they look infantile, under layers and layers of dead dead skin. No middle aged person I know really knows what they want or what they like. Some of the sharper ones even admit that they really know nothing. Some of them are so confused that it hurts. Man, then it really hurts.

I'm also scared that I'll wake up one day 20 years older with no trace of a past, everything shed and discarded somewhere along the way. Everyday I wake up feeling like the person I was yesterday is a complete stranger, that I'm rolling in change and I have no idea whom I will be tomorrow.

My didi puts it like this: "I want to grow into my skin". Whatever that means.

They say your quest in life is to return to your "natural state", the state you were born in but somewhere along the way lost. But you know why so many old people look like babies? They're so close!

But I love that you're growing older. Feels like we've stepped back for a moment to witness marked change. A small but not insignificant one. And an arbitrary but not irrelevant one. And I'm so damn touched I get to be in this picture: I get to grow old with you! Isn't that exciting? I'm pumped.

Thanks for existing for so many moments Ila. Happy birthday my love.

All this love all the way from Bangalore, for you
